Here is the Pop Cube Jingle Remix by SUGARQUOTE for PC Music’s Pop Cube Competition.

This piece features multiple self-referential objects placed in a room together to create a novel virtual space. There is an accompanying augmented reality application with the piece which allows users to insert virtual objects into this space depending on their location with respect to certain visual triggers. The piece contains a diverse set of references to major themes in the transition from modern art to the contemporary state of art. It hopes to create an emergent piece of art whose only context is art in attempt to create a tautologically-sound artwork.

In this piece I hoped to convey the failure of the futurists. Boccioni’s “Unique Froms of Continuity in Space” is stuck in place rotating over itself instead of moving forward. The aesthetics are borrowed from the Vaporwave movement in order to convey the over-saturated and accelerationist demise of the Futurists.

This piece was shown at the Tate Britain during their “All Glitched Up” show in November 2014.

I am proud to present the debut album Interference from Isomov. This album was heavily influenced by Isaac Asimov’s The Last Question and Douglas Hofstadter’s Godel, Escher, Bach.
