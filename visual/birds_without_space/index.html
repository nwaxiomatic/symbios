birds_without_space



Inspired by Brancusi’s infamous “Bird In Space”, @birds_without_space represents the 9 most “evolutionarily distinct and globally endangered” avian species in the world due to human-caused habitat loss. You can check out edgeofexistence.org for the complete list. This accompanying video piece seeks to explore the banality of such ecological horrors by flattening the traditional sculpture into a 3D render synthesizing some the largest contributors of habitat loss for these birds.

A video posted by Symbios (@symbios.wiki) on Nov 8, 2016 at 7:00am PST
